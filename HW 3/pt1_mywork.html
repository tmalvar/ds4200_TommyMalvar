My Work

This page will consist of all my projects, ranging from app prototypes to physical objects.

I made this flower for a class called Designing Interactive Experiences. The goal for this project was to visit a landmark that I found interesting, conduct research on it, and interfere with it in some way during the visit.

Instead of visiting a well-known statue or building, I decided to visit a place that meant a lot to me. The now closed restaurant, Pinoy Kabayan, was the only Filipino restaurant in the Greater Boston area. After hearing about its closure, I decided to visit the place and leave this flower I made to honor its legacy.

Using printing and magazine paper, I glued together this flower to put on the restaurant's outside ledge. When presenting, I received many compliments from peers on the thoughtfulness that went into creating this artwork. It will remain one of my favorite projects to have worked on.
